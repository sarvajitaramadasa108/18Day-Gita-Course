Why Study Bhagavad Gita?

Bhagavad Gita is one of the world's most profound spiritual texts. It provides practical guidance for managing stress, relationships, career challenges, emotions, and the deeper purpose of life.

This specially designed 18-day course presents timeless wisdom in a simple and practical manner suitable for students, professionals, homemakers and spiritual seekers.

What You Will Learn

Purpose of Life

Understand your true identity and life's deeper purpose.

Mind Management

Learn practical techniques to control the restless mind.

Stress-Free Living

Apply Gita wisdom to overcome anxiety and stress.

Karma

Understand actions, reactions and how to make better decisions.

Relationships

Develop healthier and more meaningful relationships.

Spiritual Growth

Experience inner peace and lasting happiness.
